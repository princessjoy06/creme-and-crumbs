Our Story

Crème & Crumbs was born from the warmth of a mother and daughter baking together during the quiet days of the pandemic. What began as cozy afternoons filled with flour, laughter, and love turned into a passion for bringing joy to others through beautiful, handcrafted desserts.

Our Philosophy

Every dessert we create is a small work of art. We believe in slow baking, seasonal flavors, and making people smile. Whether it’s a custom cake or a dozen cupcakes, we pour love and creativity into every detail.
